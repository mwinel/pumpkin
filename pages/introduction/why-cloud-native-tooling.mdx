# Why Cloud-Native Tooling?

With Cloud-Native Tooling an engineering team is confident they will be able to quickly deliver **value to their customers** and 
easily **extend to new features and technologies**. 

There are two major perspectives to consider while adopting cloud-native tooling: the **business** and **technical** perspectives.

From a business perspective, the adoption of cloud-native tooling represents:

- ***Agility*** - perform strategic transformations
- ***Growth*** - quickly iterate on customer feedback and
- ***Service availability*** - ensures the product is available to customers 24/7

From a technical perspective, the adoption of cloud-native tooling represents:

- ***Automation*** - release a service without human intervention
- ***Orchestration*** - introduce a container orchestrator to manage thousands of services with minimal effort and
- ***Observability*** - ability to independently troubleshoot and debug each component
